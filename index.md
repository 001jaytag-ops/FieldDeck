# Privacy Policy for FieldDeck

**Last updated: March 28, 2026**

FieldDeck ("the app", "we", "our") is developed and operated by Jayaraman Thiagarajan. This policy explains what information FieldDeck collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

---

## What Data FieldDeck Collects

### Account Information
FieldDeck uses **Sign in with Apple** for authentication. When you sign in, Apple may share your name and email address with the app. You may choose to hide your email address using Apple's private relay feature. This information is stored locally on your device only.

### Field Data You Enter
All field data you collect — including text entries, numbers, ratings, and selections — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export or share it.

### Location (GPS)
FieldDeck requests access to your device's location when you use GPS field capture within a survey. Location data is stored only within your field entries, on your device. FieldDeck does not collect or transmit your location in the background.

### Photos
If you capture or attach photos within a survey, those images are stored locally on your device within the app's data container. FieldDeck does not upload your photos to any server.

### Audio Recordings
Voice notes recorded within a survey are stored locally on your device. Audio is never transmitted externally.

### Device Sensors
FieldDeck reads your device's accelerometer and compass to measure slope and aspect in the field. Sensor data is used only in real time and is stored only when you explicitly save a field value.

---

## What FieldDeck Does Not Collect

- FieldDeck has **no analytics or tracking**. We do not use any third-party SDKs for analytics, crash reporting, or advertising.
- FieldDeck does **not share your data** with any third parties.
- FieldDeck does **not have a backend server**. There is no account database, no cloud sync, and no telemetry.

---

## Network Requests

FieldDeck makes network requests only to download survey templates from publicly hosted sources (GitHub Gist). These requests do not include any personal information or device identifiers. The app works fully offline once templates are downloaded.

---

## Data Storage and Security

All app data is stored in the app's private container on your device, protected by iOS's built-in sandboxing and encryption. Data is not accessible to other apps.

---

## Children's Privacy

FieldDeck does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not target children and contains no advertising or in-app purchases.

---

## Your Rights

Since FieldDeck stores all data locally on your device, you have full control at all times:

- **Access**: All your data is viewable within the app.
- **Deletion**: Delete individual entries within the app, or delete the app entirely to remove all data from your device.
- **Export**: Use iOS's built-in sharing features to export data you've collected.
